## Spine and Leaf Architecture
  Switches at the top are spine
  Switches at the middle are leaves
  Leaves are all connected to all spines
  Leaves are connected to servers
  Top of rack switching:
    Every rack has a leaf switch at the top of the rack
  Keeps cabling simple, redundant, and fast
  Disadvantages - additional switches may be costly
## Point-to-Point
