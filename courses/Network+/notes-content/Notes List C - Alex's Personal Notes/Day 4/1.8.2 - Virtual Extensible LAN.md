Data Center Interconnect (DCI)
  Connect multiple data centers together
  Connect and segment different customer networks
  Distribute applications everywhere
VXLAN - Virtual Extensible LAN
  Like a VLAN but designed for large service providers
  It's basically a layer 2 over layer 3 encapsulation protocol
  It's more akin to a VLAN than it is to a VPC
  Sets out to solve VLAN related issues
