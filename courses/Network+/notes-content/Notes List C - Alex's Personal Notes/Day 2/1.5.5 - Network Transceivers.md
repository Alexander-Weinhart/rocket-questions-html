Transmitter and Receiver usually in the same component
Provides a modular interface
Many different types of transceivers

## SFP and SFP+
  Small form-factor pluggable - 1Gbit/s Fiber or RJ45
  Enhanced Small Form-Factor Pluggable (SFP+) - 16Gbit/s - common with 10Gigabit ethernet

## QSFP
  Quad Small Form Factor Pluggable
  4 channel SFP = 4 1Gbps = 4Gbps
  QSFP+ is four channel SFP+ = 4 10Gbps = 40Gbps
  Extending 4 separate links over fiber
