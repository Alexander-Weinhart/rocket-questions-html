## SaaS
  The Provider manages:
    - The application
    - the network controls
    - the Operating Systems
    - the physical hosts
    - the physical network
    - the physical datacenter
  You and the provider manage:
    - identity and directory infrastructure
  You manage:
    - information and data
    - Devices accessing the service
    - your accounts and identities
## PaaS
  The Provider manages:
    - the Operating Systems
    - the physical hosts
    - the physical network
    - the physical datacenter
  You and the provider manages:
    - The identity and directory infrastructure
    - applications
    - network controls
  You manage:
    - information and data
    - Devices accessing the service
    - your accounts and identities
    
## IaaS
  Someone else manages:
    - the physical hosts
    - the physical network
    - the physical datacenter
    
  You manage:
    - information and data
    - Devices accessing the service
    - your accounts and identities
    - The identity and directory infrastructure
    - applications
    - network controls
    - the Operating Systems
