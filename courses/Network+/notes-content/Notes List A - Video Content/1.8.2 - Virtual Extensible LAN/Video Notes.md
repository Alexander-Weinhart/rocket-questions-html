## Overview

Many organizations will distribute their applications, their servers, and other devices across many different data centers. Instead of treating each of these data centers as an individual entity, it would be useful if we could have Data Center Interconnection, or DCI, a way to connect these different data centers seamlessly with each other.

## Core Ideas

- Many organizations will distribute their applications, their servers, and other devices across many different data centers.
- Instead of treating each of these data centers as an individual entity, it would be useful if we could have Data Center Interconnection, or DCI, a way to connect these different data centers seamlessly with each other.
- If you are a service provider, you may have 100 or even thousands of customers using resources that span across multiple data centers located anywhere in the world.
- And of course, we use the cloud to distribute our applications to multiple data centers that may be located in many different locations.
