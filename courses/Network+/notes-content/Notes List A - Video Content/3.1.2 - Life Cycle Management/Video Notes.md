## Core Ideas

- One of the challenges we have when installing new equipment into our data centers and our networks is that eventually this hardware and software will be outdated.
- We refer to this as end of life, or EOL.
- This is when the manufacturer of that piece of equipment decides they are no longer going to support that particular product.
- They may continue to provide security patches and updates, but they are no longer going to provide new versions of software or enhance the features associated with that product.
