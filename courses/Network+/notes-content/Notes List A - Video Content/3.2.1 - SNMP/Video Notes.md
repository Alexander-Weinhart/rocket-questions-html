## Core Ideas

- As a network administrator, you will install switches, routers, servers, firewalls and many other pieces of equipment into your infrastructure.
- Once you install that equipment, how do you ensure that it's running optimally and that there are no errors or problems that you need to address?
- One of the ways to do that is by querying this device through a protocol known as SNMP.
- This is the simple network management protocol.
