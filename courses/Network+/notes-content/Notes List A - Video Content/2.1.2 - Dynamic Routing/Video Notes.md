## Core Ideas

- Static routing requires the network administrator manually configure every route on every router in the organization.
- If you have three or four routers, that might not be a big problem.
- But if you have tens or even hundreds of routers, you might want to have a more automated way for configuring your routing tables.
- One way to do that is through the process of dynamic routing.
